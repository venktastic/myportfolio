Scan and Pay: Bringing Cashless Insurance to Non-Network Hospitals

Overview

Bridging the Cashless Gap

Non-network hospitals historically lacked cashless support, forcing patients to pay upfront and wait for reimbursements. This reduced trust and increased drop-offs.

Key Pain Points:

  • Cashless not available at non-network hospitals
  • High operational risk and fraud potential
  • Poor experience and payment failures

Discovery & Research: Speed, Trust, and Fraud Prevention

Hypothesis & Core Requirement

Enable a fast, trustworthy cashless experience by allowing users to scan and pay at any hospital while mitigating fraud and operational risk.

The Scan and Pay Feature

  • In-app Scan and Pay to initiate cashless payment flows at non-network hospitals
  • Automated verification and checks to minimize risk
  • Clear guidance and status updates throughout the flow

Results & Impact

  • Feature Adoption: 100%
  • Business Growth: +2% Revenue
  • Customer Retention: -3.5% Cancellation
  • Operational Efficiency: Reduced manual overhead with automated checks
  • Consumer Experience: Reliable cashless experience at more hospitals
